About Our Mangoes

For generations, our family orchards in Bangladesh have been cultivating the finest mangoes using traditional methods passed down through centuries.

Our mangoes are grown in the fertile lands of Rajshahi and Chapai Nawabganj, regions world-famous for producing the sweetest, most flavorful mangoes.

We harvest each mango at the perfect ripeness, ensuring they reach you with maximum flavor and freshness.

We never use artificial ripening agents or harmful chemicals - just pure, natural goodness.

Mango Seasons

Bangladeshi mangoes have a specific growing season. Plan ahead to taste these delicacies.

May

Early Season

The first harvests begin with varieties like Gopalbhog and Himsagar hitting the markets.

  • Gopalbhog
  • Himsagar
  • Khirsapat
June

Peak Season

The height of mango season brings the most coveted varieties with exceptional flavor.

  • Langra
  • Amrapali
  • Haribhanga
July

Late Season

The final phase brings larger, longer-lasting varieties for extended enjoyment.

  • Fazli
  • Ashwina
  • Kohitoor
